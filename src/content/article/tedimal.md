## TEDIMAL

TEDIMAL is a name created from Ted + Minimal.
We believe in minimal design and intuitive user experience.

Our goal is to remove unnecessary complexity and build simple, sustainable software that people enjoy using every day.
Instead of growing endlessly, we choose to stay a small and focused app studio.

We look forward to bringing you great products.
