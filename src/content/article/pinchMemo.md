## Features 
- A menu bar app for Mac 🖥️.
- A simple menu app. 
- Automatically registers copied clipboard content as a memo.
- 📌 Pin memos to the top.
- 🔎 Search functionality.
- ⌨️ Font customization.


## Requirements
- macOS 14.6 or later
